SPARROW LAB — DC & MID-ATLANTIC

Open index.html in any modern browser. No installation or internet connection is required. Audio, photos, learning state, and the full app are local. The hosted edition lives at https://lostinthehaze.net/sparrows/.

LEARNING SYSTEM
- month-aware “what is live right now” working set for the DC region
- six-pass Sparrow Compiler tutorial: structure → breast → face → habitat → season → voice
- explainable evidence key with positive/negative match reasons
- one-handed Field Mode (button or F key) for rapid observations and ranked hypotheses
- confidence-calibrated quizzes and a spaced-retrieval scheduler
- targeted drills generated from the species you miss most
- keyboard quiz controls: 1–4 answer, N advances, F opens Field Mode

SOUND
- locally stored, licensed field recordings for all 14 core species
- sound-only mystery quizzes
- a shared player with rhythm/contour visualization and explicit listening cues
- synthetic rhythm decompositions for all 14 core species
- recording-specific attribution and license links in the app

REFERENCE TOOLS
- 42 locally stored, licensed field photographs: three views for every core species
- a variation atlas covering pose, lighting, distance, sex, age, and plumage differences
- three-image species carousels with per-photo credits and license links
- photo and confusion-pair quizzes randomize the field view instead of teaching one portrait
- 14-species searchable/filterable field guide
- annotated field-mark overlays and month-by-month occurrence
- direct comparison for up to three species
- six high-value DC confusion sets, each with its own drill
- precise field-mark glossary and regional edge cases

Learning progress is stored only in localStorage in your browser. Photo and audio credits and source links are included inside the webpage. A complete, audited media register is available in attributions.html.
